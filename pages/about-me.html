Hiya, I’m Sean — a UX & Product Designer with 10 years’ experience across websites, eCommerce and digital products. I’m based in Wirral and work remotely across the UK.

I’ve delivered user-centred design for organisations including British Red Cross, Oxfam, CRUK and Peacocks, working across discovery, UX, UI, content design and delivery. Much of my recent work has involved complex, content-heavy and commerce-driven platforms, collaborating closely with developers and non-design stakeholders.

My background spans UX, UI, branding and motion, which helps me balance structure, content and visual craft. I enjoy untangling complexity, designing within real-world constraints and using evidence to inform decisions wherever possible.

Outside of work, I’m usually watching or playing football, 3D printing something unnecessary, or obsessing over a new cooking project.

Hiya, I’m Sean — a multi-disciplinary UX & Product Designer with 10 years’ experience across digital products, eCommerce, service design and branding. I’m based in Wirral and work remotely across the UK.

I’ve delivered user-centred design for organisations including British Red Cross, Oxfam, CRUK and Peacocks, spanning discovery, UX, UI, content strategy, motion and delivery. Much of my recent work has focused on complex, content-heavy and commerce-driven platforms, helping organisations simplify workflows and deliver better experiences for users.

My multi-disciplinary background allows me to balance structure, content and visual craft, untangle complexity, design within real-world constraints and use evidence to inform decisions.

Outside of work, I’m usually playing or watching football, tinkering with 3D prints, or experimenting on a new cooking project.

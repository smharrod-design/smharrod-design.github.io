Common questions
How does a monthly retainer work?

We agree on a set number of days per month and I focus that time entirely on your site. It's flexible - some months you might need a new page built, other months it's smaller updates or improvements. Retainers start from £600/month.

What if I just need a one-off piece of work?

No problem at all. Get in touch with what you need and I'll give you a straightforward quote. No hidden costs.

How quickly can you start?

Usually within a week or two depending on my current schedule. Get in touch and I'll let you know straight away.

I'm not very web savvy - is that a problem?

Not at all - in fact most of my clients aren't. I'll keep everything in plain English and handle the technical side so you don't have to.

Do you work with Magento sites?

Yes - I have extensive experience designing and improving Magento ecommerce sites specifically, which is relatively rare for a freelancer.
